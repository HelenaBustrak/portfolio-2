Solarsmart

18.12.2022
UI design, Semester Project

This is a UI design of an imaginitive company called Solar Smart. I was tasked with creating a design guide, a website prototype and a design system. The company provides solar panels to homes, businesses and communities. When creating the design I took inspiration from the sun and renewable energy. The main color of the design is a calm, neon green which I felt was an accurate representation of the company.

Paw Haven

10.04.2023
UI design, Course: Prototyping

Paw Haven is another imaginitive company which was the course assignment for the course Prototyping. The company is a local animal rescue for dogs and cats. When I did some research on animal rescues websites I saw a lot of red and blue, and decided to stick with the color blue as it creates a more calming effect on the user.

Tree Life

UI design, Course: Design Foundations
25.09.2023

TreeLife is once again another imaginitive company. This was one of the first course assignment I did in my UI design school year. I focused a lot on making the website prototype as accessible as possible, while also explaining TreeLife's mission in an easy, understandable way. TreeLife's mission is to teach users how to plant a tree, and why it is important.
